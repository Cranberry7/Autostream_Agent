# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream

AutoStream is a SaaS platform offering automated video editing tools for content creators.
It uses AI to help YouTubers, TikTokers, Instagram Reels creators, and podcasters produce
professional-quality videos in minutes — without needing editing expertise.

---

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan — $29/month

The Basic Plan is designed for individual creators just getting started.

**Features:**
- Up to 10 videos per month
- Maximum resolution: 720p (HD)
- Automated cuts and transitions
- Background music library access
- Standard export formats (MP4, MOV)
- Email support (response within 48 hours)

**Ideal for:** Hobbyist creators, small channels, freelancers on a budget.

---

### Pro Plan — $79/month

The Pro Plan is built for serious, professional content creators.

**Features:**
- Unlimited videos per month
- Maximum resolution: 4K (Ultra HD)
- AI-powered automatic captions and subtitles
- Advanced AI scene detection and smart trimming
- Multi-platform export optimization (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Priority rendering queue
- Team collaboration (up to 3 seats)
- 24/7 live chat and priority phone support

**Ideal for:** Full-time creators, agencies, brands, and YouTube channels with 10k+ subscribers.

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy

- AutoStream offers a **7-day free trial** on all plans.
- **No refunds are issued after the 7-day trial period** has ended.
- Cancellations made within the 7-day window will receive a full refund.
- Downgrades or plan changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Support Policy

- **24/7 support** (live chat + priority phone) is available **exclusively on the Pro plan**.
- Basic plan users receive email-only support with a 48-hour response window.
- Both plans have access to the self-service help center and community forums.

### Data & Privacy

- AutoStream does not store raw video footage on its servers after processing.
- All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Users retain full intellectual property rights to their content.

### Acceptable Use

- AutoStream may not be used to produce content that violates copyright law.
- Spam or automated bulk content generation for deceptive purposes is prohibited.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro anytime?**
A: Yes, upgrades take effect immediately and you're billed the prorated difference.

**Q: Does the Pro plan include a free trial?**
A: Yes, all new accounts get a 7-day free trial regardless of plan selected.

**Q: Is there an annual billing option?**
A: Annual billing gives you 2 months free (Basic: $290/yr, Pro: $790/yr). Coming soon.

**Q: What video formats does AutoStream support?**
A: Input: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV. Output: MP4, MOV, WebM.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. No lock-in.
